User Experience

The aim for our project is to help people's life, especially for itchy on their face. So, user experience is always the first place in our development.

- The process of using our algorithm is simple, user just need to look at the camera, click the calibration button, then the algorithm will work automatically.

- All the facial data will directly show to the user, they will easily find out what is happening of the algorithm.

- A user manual is provided for guiding the user easily get on with the algorithm.

- Twerk of eyebrows and conner of the lip are friendly to all type of users​

Functionality

All our work focus on one job, facial itchy detection. Our algorithm only focus on this one function. We made it efficient, logic clear and easy for user to use.

Stability-Efficiency

Because our algorithm is based on capturing real-time video frames through a camera, the lighting of the user's environment, the distance from the camera, and the angle of deflection of the user's face can have a certain impact on the algorithm's results. In the worst situation, the algorithm won't be able to detect the movement of the face. But most of the situation with good user circumstance, our algorithm works perfectly.

Setting aside some unstable factors, our algorithm can efficiently and accurately identify indications of human facial itching

Compatibility

Our algorithm is efficient and simple, which can be ported for use in various environments, not only for itchy detection, in future. It is the best solution we can think of for human's facial itch detection.

Maintainability

Our algorithm can be viewed as an entire module that can be ported to various terminals. We can place it on a network server and make it a remote service. It can also be implanted into local devices to expand the functions of intelligent devices to facilitate users' lives.

This algorithm do not need further cost, all the library and dependencies are open sources. Our algorithm has extremely strong scalability which is easy to develop new features in future.

Project Management

This project has been well managed. Each of our team members follows their target. All the works are accomplished follow the Gantt chart we made at preparation stage. We set up weekly meeting for tracing the progress of the project. All in all, we did a really good job and finished the requirements from our customer nice and clean.

Future Work​

1. Optimizing algorithms or using other more advanced algorithms allows our algorithm to be used under more severe usage conditions, such as insufficient lighting, continuous user movement, and occlusion of the user's face, etc.

2. Develop more features, not only detecting the itchy on human's face. Upgrade our facial gesture detection algorithm, enable it to recognize more instructions and commands.

3. Integrate our algorithm into the RE-1 robot to instruct the robot to help hemiplegic patients grasp facial itching more easily.

4. Deploy the ItchyAI on a network server and turn our algorithm into a service for remote use by the public.
